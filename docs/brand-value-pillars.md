# Catharsis Body Art · Brand Value Pillars

## How to Use This Document
- Anchor copywriting, visual tone, and service decisions in the promises below.
- Stress-test new ideas: if a campaign, policy, or feature weakens a pillar, revise it.
- Share with new hires and partners so the experience stays consistent across channels.

## Experience That Calms & Empowers
- **Sanctuary-first studios:** Private, spa-like bays with curated lighting, scent, and playlists so guests can exhale.
- **Guided rituals:** Artists lead with empathy—explaining steps, checking comfort, and pacing sessions to the client.
- **Support beyond the chair:** Complimentary consults, jewelry sizing, and aftercare coaching remove guesswork.

## Artistry Rooted in Craftsmanship
- **Custom narratives:** Every tattoo and piercing is designed around the individual—no flash-only shortcuts.
- **Collaborative studio:** Tattooists and piercers co-create cohesive “body stories,” sharing references and techniques.
- **Showcase the finish:** Portfolio photography highlights healed results and lightbox galleries feature real clients.

## Safety Without Compromise
- **Medical-grade protocol:** Single-use disposables, weekly autoclave spore testing, and documented sterilization logs.
- **Transparent guidance:** Saniderm-first healing plans, written aftercare, and a direct hotline for questions.
- **Fully licensed:** Insured, inspected, and proud to make compliance visible to every guest.

## Transparent Partnership from Booking to Touch-Up
- **Open communication:** Online booking calendar, responsive channels, and clear SLAs keep clients informed.
- **No surprises:** Honest estimates, deposit policies, and session planning eliminate uncertainty.
- **Commitment honored:** Free touch-ups within three months and loyalty perks reward returning clients.

## Community Connection
- **Local collaborations:** Partnerships with AFSP Indiana, Willow Center, and neighboring creatives extend our impact.
- **Merch with meaning:** Apparel and accessories reinforce our “You Matter” ethos and fund community work.
- **Radically inclusive:** First-timers, veterans, all bodies, and all stories are celebrated—no exceptions.
