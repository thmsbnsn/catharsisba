# Catharsis Body Art · Value Pillars

## Experience that calms and empowers
- Private, spa-like bays with curated lighting and music so every guest can breathe, relax, and focus on the ritual.
- Artists lead with empathy, giving clear expectations before needles touch skin.
- Complimentary consults, jewelry sizing, and aftercare guidance eliminate guesswork.

## Artistry rooted in craftsmanship
- Custom designs and precision piercings tailored to the individual—no templated flash-only approach.
- Continuous collaboration between tattooists and piercers to deliver cohesive body art stories.
- Portfolio-worthy photography and lightbox galleries showcase healed results, not just day-one work.

## Safety without compromise
- Hospital-grade sterilization, single-use disposables, weekly autoclave spore testing.
- Clear aftercare instructions, Saniderm-first healing, and direct hotline for post-session questions.
- Licensed, insured, and inspected studio with documented protocols accessible to clients.

## Transparent partnership from booking to touch-up
- NeetoCal scheduling, Supabase-powered contact form, and rapid response SLAs keep communication tight.
- Honest pricing estimates, deposit policies, and session planning reduce surprises.
- Free touch-ups within three months and loyalty perks reward repeat clients.

## Community connection
- Collaborations with local partners (AFSP Indiana, Willow Center, etc.) extend impact beyond the chair.
- Merchandise and events promote self-worth and give back to the Brownsburg community.
- Inclusive space welcoming first-timers, veterans, and every body in between.
